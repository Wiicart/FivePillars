Five Pillars

Nursery and Primary School

About

Founding:

We were founded in Moshi, Tanzania in 2020.
We now accomodate disabled children.

Low Income:

We bridge the education gap by providing quality education to children who come from low income households.
Low income students are eligible to have their fees waived, and we provide additional support to them.

Background


Currently African children deserve special attention.

Decreases in infant mortality has made the population distribution of sub-saharan Africa extremely skewed towards children, with estimates finding that 46% of the population is under 15.

These children have great potential but the socio-economic situation in Africa is not prepared for them.

As a school, Five Pillars aims to increase access to education for Moshi's youth, regardless of ethnic or religious background.

Objectives

  • To contribute to the education of disabled children, who are increasingly being exposed to to the negative impacts of poverty.

  • To promote an atmosphere where children feel loved, so they can grow with love and self respect and reach their greatest potential.

  • To protect children.

  • To create educational opportunities for low income students.

  • To strengthen programs that help children reach their full potential.
